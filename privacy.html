Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

ClipDrop ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web application and services (the "Service") that integrate with the TikTok API.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you log in via TikTok, we receive your TikTok display name, username, avatar, and open ID as provided by TikTok's API.
  • Content Data: Videos and media files you upload through our Service for posting to TikTok.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our Service, including timestamps, features used, and posting activity.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, IP address, and cookies necessary for the Service to function.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity via TikTok OAuth.
  • To publish video content to your TikTok account on your behalf.
  • To display your posting history and account information within the Service.
  • To improve and maintain the Service.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. TikTok API Data Usage

We use TikTok's API solely to enable you to publish content to your TikTok account. We do not sell, share, or use your TikTok data for advertising purposes. All data obtained through TikTok's API is handled in accordance with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and Platform Policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service. Video files uploaded for posting are temporarily stored and deleted after successful publication or within 24 hours, whichever comes first. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with: TikTok (to publish your content as requested), service providers essential to operating the Service, and law enforcement when required by law.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Authentication tokens are encrypted and stored securely. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, request correction or deletion of your data, revoke TikTok permissions at any time through your TikTok account settings, and withdraw consent to data processing.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our application.
